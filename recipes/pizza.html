Pizza de Palmitos

Description

Pizza de Palmitos is a great and simple pizza dish anyone can cook!

Ingredients

  • PrePizza
  • Cheese
  • Salsa Golf
  • Palmitos
  • Tomato Sauce

    • Steps

    1. Put some tomato sauce on your prepizza
    2. Put some cheese on your prepizza
    3. Put some palmitos on your pizza
    4. Put some salsa golf
    5. Put pizza in the oven to cook
    6. Enjoy with a cold beer!
