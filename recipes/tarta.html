Tarta de Jamon y Queso

Description

This is a very easy to do dish with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Jam
  • Pascualina

    • Steps

    1. Mix eggs in a bowl
    2. Mix cheese and jam with eggs
    3. Put everything into the pasualina and close it in a tarta shape
    4. Put tarta in the oven to cook
    5. Enjoy with a cold honey flavored beer!
