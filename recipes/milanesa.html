Milanesa

Description

This is a tipical dish from Argentina called milanesa.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Lemon optional

    • Steps

    1. Prepare meat. Cut it in loafs of meat
    2. Put eggs in a bowl and mix them
    3. One by one, bathe the meat with the eggs
    4. Put the bathed meat on bread
    5. Put milanesas in the oven to cook
    6. Enjoy with or without lemon
